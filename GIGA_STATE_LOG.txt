--- TENNIS (ml_pipeline_tennis.py) ---
API: TheSportsDB (free key "3")
  GET https://www.thesportsdb.com/api/v1/json/3/searchteams.php?t=PLAYER_NAME
  GET https://www.thesportsdb.com/api/v1/json/3/searchplayers.php?p=PLAYER_NAME
  GET https://www.thesportsdb.com/api/v1/json/3/eventslast.php?id=TEAM_ID
Method: LIVE feature engineering at prediction time (NOT pre-trained on historical)
  - Fetches last 5 matches per player from TheSportsDB
  - Calculates win rate % (0-100) as "form" feature
  - If team/player not found → fallback to 50.0 (neutral)
  - Passes [Home_Form, Away_Form, Form_Diff] through XGBClassifier
Model: Synthetic training on 10,000 simulated samples (sigmoid distributions)
       Trained once on startup, saved as: tennis_model.pkl
Edge threshold for value bet: > 2.0% above implied probability
